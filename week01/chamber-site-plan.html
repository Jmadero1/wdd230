Mar del Plata La Feliz

Our mission

The purpose of the Mar del Plata website is to promote tourism by showcasing the city's attractions, events, and businesses, enticing visitors to explore its offerings. Additionally, the site supports local businesses by featuring a directory and serving as a platform for promoting economic growth and community engagement within Mar del Plata.

Excursion to Playa Grande

Enjoy a sunny day at one of Mar del Plata's most popular beaches. Perfect for relaxing, swimming, and water sports.

Harbor Tour

Discover Mar del Plata's fishing activity by visiting the harbor, where you can see boats, fishermen, and enjoy fresh seafood at local restaurants.

Stroll through the Historic Center

Walk through the picturesque streets of Mar del Plata's historic center, discover its colonial architecture, and visit its museums and art galleries.

Whale Watching

Embark on an exciting excursion to spot whales in waters near Mar del Plata. A unique experience in contact with nature.

Visit to Mar del Plata Aquarium

Explore marine life at the Mar del Plata Aquarium, where you can see a wide variety of fish, sharks, and marine animals in interactive exhibits.

Lighthouse Tour

Visit Mar del Plata's iconic lighthouses and enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the coast and the sea from the top of these historic buildings.
